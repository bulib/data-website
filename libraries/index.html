Boston University Libraries

How the libraries can help you with your data.

Consultations

Need help? Schedule a one-on-one consultation.

Librarians can help you with writing a data management plan, depositing your data into a data repository, finding relevant data sets, and organizing your data for analysis. We can also connect you with other partners on-campus to help with more in-depth data analysis or publishing your research.

Request

Workshops

The Libraries offer a range of workshops on topics like data management, digital scholarship, digital publishing, and selected programming topics.

Open Science Framework

Coming Soon!! The Open Science Framework (OSF) is project management tool developed by the Center for Open Science. With integrations to GitHub, figshare, and Google Drive (to name a few) the OSF can help you manage your work, archive your data, and control access.

Our Guides

Use our guides to learn independently.

The Libraries have a number of guides to help you get started with your project. Topics include citing data, selecting a data repository, writing a data management plan, and using the DMPTool. These guides are a great place to start whenever you are ready and from wherever you are located.
