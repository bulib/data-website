    On-Campus Partners

    BEDAC

    The Biostatistics and Epidemiology Data Analytics Center (BEDAC) assists with questionnaire and data collection, study protocols, data entry/management, and statistical, database, and web-based programming.

    Learn More

    IS&T

    Information Services and Technology (IS&T) supports research computing, information security, and our IT infrastructure. IS&T can help you program, access the Shared Computing Cluster, and store your data.

    Learn More

    Libraries

    The BU Libraries help researchers write data management plans, find archival storage solutions for their data, and access open data. Library staff can help you publish your data and make it accessible to others.
